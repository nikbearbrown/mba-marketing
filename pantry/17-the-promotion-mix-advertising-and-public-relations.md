## Core mechanisms

- **Advertising** = paid, non-personal communication identifying brand/organization.
- **Public relations** = creating and maintaining favorable public image; output is publicity (earned media).
- **Six-step advertising plan:** objectives → push/pull → budget → strategy → execute → assess.
- **Push vs. pull strategies** — push through channels vs. pull customers through advertising.
- **Impressions formula:** Rating × Target Population × Number of Ads Run.

## Vocabularies

- **Advertising types by objective:** informative / persuasive / comparative / reminder.
- **Product vs. institutional advertising.**
- **Advertising KPIs:** brand recognition, brand awareness, impressions, reach, frequency, GRPs.
- **PR tools:** press releases, sponsorships, events, charitable activities, spokespeople, crisis communication.
- **Earned vs. paid media.**
