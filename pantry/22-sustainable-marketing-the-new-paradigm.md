## Core mechanisms

- **Sustainable marketing** (Kotler) = meeting present consumers' needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet theirs.
- **Three pillars (ESG):** Environmental / Social / Economic (Governance) — also "planet, people, profits."
- **Stakeholder approach** (sustainable) vs. **customer approach** (traditional) — broader set of interests.
- **Six benefits:** enhanced brand recognition / reduced costs / improved effectiveness / easier regulatory compliance / waste minimization / enhanced ROI.
- **Five principles:** consumer-oriented / customer-value / innovative / mission-driven / societal.

## Closing arguments anchored

- Marketing that produces customer value while sustaining the systems that customer value depends on produces both customer value and societal value simultaneously.
- The frameworks aren't in tension; they reinforce each other when done well.
