## Core moves

**The audience-layer frame.** Unit 1 = "what is value" (the foundation layer). Unit 2 = "who values it" (the audience layer). Unit 3 = "how to deliver it" (the delivery layer). The audience layer asks: *whose perception of $B$ are we trying to move?*

**The three categories of marketplace knowledge.** (1) Buyer behavior — how decisions get made (Ch 5–6, splitting consumer from business markets). (2) STP — segmentation, targeting, positioning (Ch 7). (3) Research — empirical methods that ground assertions in evidence (Ch 8).

**The two extending contexts.** Global (Ch 9) and diverse domestic (Ch 10) — the strategic frameworks still apply, but the inputs are harder to gather and easier to get wrong.
