## Core mechanisms

- **Direct marketing** = communication tools engaging individual consumers with calls to action; traditional + digital forms.
- **Social media marketing** = platform-based engagement with content.
- **SEM vs. SEO** = paid vs. organic search.
- **Mobile marketing** = location, push notifications, app-based engagement, SMS.
- **Influencer marketing** = paid/sponsored content from individuals with audiences.

## Vocabularies

**Traditional direct marketing:** direct mail / catalogs / telemarketing / infomercials.
**Digital direct marketing:** email / SMS / push notifications / website-driven response.
**Major social platforms:** YouTube + Facebook (broad demographic), Instagram + Snapchat + TikTok (under 30), LinkedIn (B2B), Twitter/X (real-time).
**Digital metrics:** website traffic / traffic by source / sales funnel / CTR / conversion rate / bounce rate / CPC / CPA / engagement / ROI.
