## Core mechanisms

**Four characteristics of services:** intangibility / inseparability / variability (heterogeneity) / perishability.

**Service-profit chain (Harvard 1990s):** internal service quality → employee satisfaction → loyalty/productivity → value → customer satisfaction → customer loyalty → profitability.

**Services Marketing Triangle:** firm / employee / customer with three marketing types — external (firm→customer), internal (firm→employee), interactive (employee→customer).

**Gap Model of Service Quality (1985, 5 gaps):** knowledge / policy / delivery / communication / customer.

**7Ps extension:** standard 4Ps + People + Process + Physical Evidence.

## Vocabularies

- **People-based services** — unskilled (parking, babysitters, janitors) / skilled (plumbers, caterers, hairstylists) / professionals (doctors, attorneys, professors, accountants).
- **Equipment-based services** — vending, ATMs, automated car washes, cloud software.
- **Front-of-house** vs. **back-of-house** — restaurant metaphor for customer-facing vs. supporting roles.
