## Core mechanisms

- **Retailing** = selling goods/services to end consumers; final link in distribution chain.
- **Wholesaling** = buying bulk from manufacturers, selling to retailers.
- **Four retail strategy decisions:** pricing / location / merchandise / communications.
- **Three wholesaler types:** full-service / limited-service / brokers and agents.

## Vocabularies

**Store retailers:** department stores / specialty / supermarkets / convenience / discount / off-price.
**Non-store retailers:** e-commerce / direct selling / vending / direct mail.
**Trends:** omnichannel / e-commerce growth / DTC / experiential retail / sustainability positioning.
