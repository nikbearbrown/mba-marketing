## Core mechanisms

- **Price = value exchanged between buyer and seller.** Oldest records 3300 BCE Uruk clay tablets.
- **Five Cs of pricing:** cost / customers / channels / competition / compatibility.
- **Five-step pricing process:** determine objectives → estimate demand → estimate costs → analyze external environment → select strategy.
- **Break-even formula:** $\text{Break-Even Units} = \frac{\text{Fixed Costs}}{\text{Unit Price} - \text{Variable Unit Cost}}$.

## Vocabularies

**Seven pricing objectives:** customer value / cost / sales orientation / market share / target return / competition / customer-driven.

**New-product pricing strategies:** skimming (Sony PS3 $599 → $299) / penetration (Netflix late-1990s DVD-by-mail) / break-even.

**Existing-product pricing:** product line pricing (Unilever portfolio) / captive product pricing (printers + ink) / bundle pricing (fast-food meals) / psychological / odd-even pricing ($19.95 vs. $50.00).

**Ethics violations:** price fixing / deceptive price advertising / predatory pricing (Walmart 2000 Wisconsin case) / price discrimination / monopoly gouging.
