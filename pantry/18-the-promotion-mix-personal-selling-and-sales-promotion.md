## Core mechanisms

- **Personal selling** = one-on-one company-rep + customer interaction; best for high-price, complex, customizable products.
- **Sales promotion** = short-term incentives for purchase; direct call to action.
- **Seven-step selling process:** prospecting & qualifying → preapproach → approach → presentation → handling objections → close → follow-up.

## Vocabularies

**Personal selling roles:**
- Order takers (inside / outside)
- Order getters (proactive)
- Missionary sellers (build relationships, educate without direct sale)

**Sales force structures:** territorial / product / customer / matrix.

**Consumer-oriented sales promotion:** coupons / samples / contests / sweepstakes / BOGO / refunds / rebates / loyalty programs.

**Trade-oriented sales promotion:** push allowances / cooperative advertising / trade shows / dealer incentives / in-store displays.

## Recurring named cases

- Hilton Hotels & Resorts — 1919 Conrad Hilton; 6,800+ hotels; Hampton Inn / Conrad / DoubleTree sub-brands; Silver/Gold/Diamond rewards tiers; Hilton Grand Vacations (HGV) sales process.
- TalEx — HR firm relationship selling.
- HelloFresh — 16 free meals direct-mail coupon.
- McDonald's — "Free fries on Friday" app promotion.
- Sargento — BOGO grocery promotion.

## Common ethical failure modes

- Expense account / credit card misuse.
- Sales data inflation.
- Bribes and kickbacks (FCPA from Ch 6 applies internationally).
- Dishonest product claims.
- Slander of competitors.
- Misleading sales promotions (FTC standards from Ch 15).
- Bait-and-switch.
