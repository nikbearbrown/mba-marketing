10 total. Within the chapter's 6–10 target band per voice spec; chapter 2 is intentionally at the upper bound because it sets up the book's central frameworks.

## Production notes

- **Image #1 (Gatorade shelf photo).** Do not reproduce actual Gatorade campaign frames. Use editorial photography or commissioned illustration. Caption acknowledges Gatorade's GOAT Camp campaign without depicting it.
- **All numerical data in figures must trace to the same source as the chapter text.** No invented numbers. Designers receive the cited source alongside the brief.
- **Inline comments include the *why*, not just the *what*.** The pedagogical role line is part of the brief — designers should optimize the figure for that role, not just for visual appeal.
- **Captions are voice work, not labels.** Captions read aloud and pass the ear test. Drafted alongside the figure, not bolted on after.

---

## Conventions across the book (locked from this chapter)

- **6–10 placeholders per chapter.** Long, dense chapters at the upper bound; short or single-mechanism chapters at the lower.
- **Mix of all five types in every chapter.** A chapter with no DIAGRAM or no TABLE probably hasn't visualized its core relationships.
- **Each placeholder marks placement, type, brief, and pedagogical purpose.** All four in one HTML comment.
- **The images manifest file is index-only.** Briefs live in the chapter; the manifest is for production tracking.
